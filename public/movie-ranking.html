The Best Movies According to Yixin

My Current Top Tree Movies

Daisy

Been watching this movie over 20 times both solo and with my friends. Very poetic yet nerve-racking love story/action movie.

The Curious Case of Benjamin Button

A classic. Artisitc shooting and editing, beautiful scenes, astonishing make-up work, outstanding actors and above all, revealing life stories to provoke deep thinking about oneself.

Gone with the Wind

Best book to movie adaptation. Music, scenes, clothing, actors, etc. everthing is marvelous.
